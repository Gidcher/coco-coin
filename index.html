CoCo: The Meme Revolution in Web3

Coco ($COCO) was a meme coin launched on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) in 2024, designed as a SocialFi token that combined community engagement with financial incentives. Initially built on hype and viral marketing, it gained traction but later faced internal conflicts among the team and investors. This led to a significant price drop, loss of trust, and a major decline in market cap.

Despite these setbacks, COCO's strong community refused to let it die, leading to the birth of CocoCTO — a community-led revival movement.

What is Coco CTO?

Coco CTO is the community-driven rebirth of the Coco ecosystem. After the original project struggled, passionate holders and builders took over to reshape its future. Coco CTO isn’t just a new version of COCO — it represents decentralized leadership, sustainable growth, and real utility.
